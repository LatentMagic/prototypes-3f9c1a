# Circlists — What It Is

Stable one-pager for designers and collaborators. Durable essence only — no delivery status, no spec detail, no PR trail. Live product memory (volatile) lives in the wiki Circlists entity.

## The product

A **shared multiplayer reading queue**. Atomic unit = a URL.

Core loop: someone drops a link → it appears in everyone's feed → each person reads it externally → marks it read in their own state. A **communal library with individual read-state**.

## Who it's for

Small trusted circles — a few people who already share links and want one calm shared place for them. One person (the **champion**) funds a space; everyone they invite reads free. Spaces stay small by design — the whole circle, one price.

## How it's sold

Flat **per-space subscription** — one space, one price, up to a small circle of members. Champion pays; invitees free. Billing handled by a Merchant of Record.

## Feel — emotional intent

The felt target, in three moments:

- **Confident release** — giving a link to the circle.
- **Quiet cohesion** — receiving what the circle shares.
- **Composed relief** — clearing what's read.

**Calm is the floor.** Anxiety, performance pressure, and FOMO are avoided by design. Direction: **Pulse Modernist** (see [BRANDING.md](BRANDING.md)).

## Deliberately NOT

What it is not — as durable as what it is:

- No single-player / personal space — it's communal by nature.
- No "who read it" avatars or social-proof signals.
- No digest cards, no feeds-of-feeds.
- URLs only — no notes, files, or non-link items.
- Delete is **everyone-delete** (a shared moderation action), not a private dismissal.
