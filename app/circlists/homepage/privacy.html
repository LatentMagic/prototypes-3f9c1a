Privacy

Last revised · 06 May 2026

Circlists is a reading queue for small trusted squads. This notice explains what we collect, why, and how to reach us. Plain language, no dark patterns.

What we collect

On this marketing site, we collect basic, privacy-respecting analytics: pages viewed, approximate location derived from IP address, and browser user-agent string. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any account information here — there is no account to create.

  • Pages viewed and referrer (where you came from).
  • IP address, used only to derive approximate country / region, then discarded.
  • Browser user-agent string, used for browser-support diagnostics.

When you email support@circlists.com, we receive your email address and whatever you write to us. We use it only to reply.

Email collection

If you submit your email, we store it and use it once to notify you when Circlists opens. We won't share it or send anything else. Email support@circlists.com to delete it.

Cookies

We use a single, first-party analytics cookie to count unique visits. It contains no personal information. We do not run third-party advertising, tracking, or social-pixel cookies.

Contact

Questions, deletion requests, or anything else: write to support@circlists.com. We respond within five working days.

Updates

If this notice changes materially, the revised date above will move and we will note the change here.
