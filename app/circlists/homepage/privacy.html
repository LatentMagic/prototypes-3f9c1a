Privacy

Last revised · 16 July 2026

Circlists is a shared list for your circle: share links with everyone, consume at your own pace. This notice explains what we collect, why, and how to reach us.

What we collect

We use PostHog to count visits and see which parts of the page get used. Visits stay anonymous: no names, no emails, no location, and no third-party advertising or tracking. The data is stored on servers in the EU, and we keep it only as long as it's useful for understanding how the site is used.

Our lawful basis is legitimate interest — understanding how the site's used so we can make it better. You can object any time: email support@circlists.com.

When you email support@circlists.com, we receive your email address and whatever you write to us. We use it only to reply.

Cookies and storage

Our analytics stores nothing on your device — no cookies.

The interactive demo on the home page does save its own state on your device — which screen you're on, which circle you opened — so it doesn't reset while you look around. It never leaves your device, and clearing your browser's site data removes it.

Contact

Questions, deletion requests, or anything else: write to support@circlists.com. We respond within five working days.

Updates

If this notice changes materially, the revised date above will move and we will note the change here.
